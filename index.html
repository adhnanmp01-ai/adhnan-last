How We Work
A process built for
global precision.
01
Inquiry & Sourcing
You share your requirement — quantity, spec, destination. We identify the right verified suppliers and source the goods.
02
Quotation & Agreement
We present competitive pricing with full transparency. A proforma invoice is issued and payment terms agreed.
03
Quality Inspection
Goods undergo rigorous third-party quality checks. Certificates of origin, lab reports, and phytosanitary documents are prepared.
04
Customs & Documentation
We handle all export documentation — Bill of Lading, commercial invoice, packing list, LC compliance — end-to-end.
05
Shipment & Delivery
Your cargo departs from Kochi or Trivandrum — by sea or air — and we track it all the way to your door.
